Show This Japan

Simple Japanese phrase cards you can quickly show on your phone in Japan.

⚠️Problem

Japan is amazing, but small situations can feel stressful when you do not speak Japanese yet.

🧭What this project does

Each phrase card shows Japanese, romaji, and English so you can understand the phrase and show it when you need help.

🗂️Sample phrase card

Japanese ○○駅はどこですか？

Romaji ○○ eki wa doko desu ka?

English Where is ○○ station?
