Show This Japan

Simple Japanese phrase cards you can quickly show on your phone in Japan.

⚠️Japan is amazing, but small situations can feel stressful.

Getting lost, feeling sick, ordering food, or asking for help can be difficult when you do not speak Japanese.

🧭What this project does

Show This Japan creates simple phrase cards with Japanese, romaji, and English. You can save them and show them on your phone when you need help.

🪜How it works

Each card shows:

  • Japanese
  • Romaji
  • English meaning

So you can understand the phrase and show it to someone easily.

🗂️Sample phrase card

Japanese 近くの病院はどこですか？

Romaji chikaku no byouin wa doko desu ka?

English Where is the nearest hospital?
